Why St.Gallen

A city with a unique quality of life!

A city you can truly call home! It boasts a wide range of facilities for education, sports, art and culture, plus many other opportunities in and around its centre. All in all, a city with an urban edge, yet still in the middle of the countryside.

With a population of 80,000, St.Gallen, Switzerland’s eighth largest city, is the economic, political, social and cultural metropolis of eastern Switzerland. Its 1,400 years of history blend with urban creativity, an innovative economy and an unspoilt landscape. Feel the quality of life eastern Switzerland has to offer at every turn!

CAFÉS

My favourite cafés in St.Gallen

Kafi Franz

A beautiful café with lovely interiour where you can spend hours and hours.

Adress

Linsebühlstrasse 35, 9000 St. Gallen

What I like about it

This café feels like a beautiful livingroom. The coffee is great and the food is delicious.
Insider tip: try St.Gallens best cakes there. The brunch on the weekend is also a must-do.

Check it out

Blumenmarkt

Right in the city center there is the well-known Blumenmarkt. There are many locals going there almost every single day.

Adress

Marktplatz 25, 9000 St. Gallen

What I like about it

Any time of the day it's a nice place to enjoy a coffee, meet up with somebody, go for a drink or even a night out. For lunch you can have salads or focaccia.


Check it out

Oya Bar Kafé

A nordic café that gives you the "hygge"-feeling.

Adress

Schwertgasse 27, 9000 St. Gallen

What I like about it

The nordic interiour makes Oya a very cozy place to have a coffee, a piece of cake or a homemade Swedish treat. In the evening Oya turns into a bar where you can get delicious drinks and enjoy music, often with a live DJ.


Check it out
